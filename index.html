What is MPTC

MPTC stands for Missionary preparatory Training Course. It is a Missional Discipleship program which has various expositions for Professionals and Business people. It is a movement that God inspires the heart of students to understand His mission which has a ripple effect impacting every person they come in touch with, through the way they live (Missional Living) & the work they do(business/any profession/homemaking/studies etc. - Missional Practice ) As the world evolves rapidly in trends such as Globalization and new work culture, the understanding of Missions also evolves under the sovereign leading of God in such a way that every Christ's Disciple is called to be a Missionary in his/ her own work place to testify about God's Glory by the way they live their life as well by sharing of the Gospel (John 17: 20 and 20: 21) As modern days' sons of Issachar, we could sense that it's not the evolving trend of Globalization, but it is God who is bringing multi ethnic people into one place i.e. in the midst of HIS people to display His Glory in the Economic world. This we see as God's mission in these last days. So, at MPTC we equip the children of God to participate in God's Mission based on the below curriculum.

Why MPTC

While REFORMATION started in 1517, one of the important impacts it did was to break the Secular Sacred divide and Laity Clergy divide. However, these divides have somehow subtly come up again and is now ruling the churches vehemently. It destroys God's mission in the Modern day world. Also, the basic doctrine of Reformation is gone off and enemy has brought in new doctrines in churches that make believers to seek God for worldly things rather than seeking God for His Glory, which is the heart of Missions. So, with this burden MPTC works with a 4M VISION. 1M- Missional church - Not a Consumer church: 2M- Ministry enhancing church - Not a one man show or Performance based church 3M- Multiplying church - Not a Mega church 4M- Materialism free churches - Not Materialistic church We understand today's church is in need of REFORMATION which includes REVIVAL. With this VISION, MPTC works among believers to make them understand the heart of God and work towards them.
